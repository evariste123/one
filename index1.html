Strategies, standards, resources to make
the Web accessible to people with disabilities

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
develops international standards for the Web:
HTML, CSS, and many more.

The W3C Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI)
develops standards and support materials to help you
understand and implement accessibility.

You can use W3C WAI resources
to make your websites, applications,
and other digital creations more accessible
and usable to everyone
